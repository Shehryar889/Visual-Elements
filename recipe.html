Biryani

The best rice dish in the world.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Red spices
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Steps to prepare recipe

  1. Boil the rice in a cointainer
  2. Marinate the chicken using oil, spices and salt
  3. Mix the chicken and rice and ligtly heat them for few minutes

It is my favorite recipe because it gives the perfect flavor with the mix of rice and chicken.
